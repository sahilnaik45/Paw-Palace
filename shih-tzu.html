Grooming Shih-Tzu
  • Brush and bath

    Brush your Shih Tzu’s coat weekly to maintain its shine and health and reduce shedding. Occasional baths using mild shampoo will keep them clean and fresh.

  • Keep their ears clean

    Check and clean ears regularly with a mild ear-cleaning solution and a cotton ball to promote ear health.

  • Brush their coat regularly to reduce shedding.

    Trim their nails regularly to prevent discomfort and ensure their paw pads are clean and free from any issues.
